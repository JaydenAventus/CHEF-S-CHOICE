Bitterballen

Origin: Netherlands 🇳🇱

Crispy, golden-fried breaded meat croquettes filled with a savory ragout. These beloved Dutch appetizers are traditionally served as a bar snack with mustard. The combination of crispy exterior and creamy, meaty interior makes them an irresistible classic.

Seared Duck Breast

Origin: France 🇫🇷

Pan-seared duck breast with a golden, crispy skin and perfectly cooked medium-rare center. This elegant French classic is often served with a rich orange or cherry sauce, accompanied by fresh vegetables and seasonal garnishes. A sophisticated dish that's surprisingly simple to prepare.

Tropical Fruit Bowl

Origin: International 🌍

A vibrant and refreshing combination of fresh tropical fruits including strawberries, kiwi, blueberries, and mandarin oranges, topped with fresh mint leaves. Perfect as a healthy breakfast, dessert, or light snack. Packed with vitamins, antioxidants, and natural sweetness.
